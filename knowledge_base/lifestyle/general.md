# General Health and Lifestyle Recommendations

## BMI (Body Mass Index)
BMI is an estimate of body fat based on height and weight.
- Normal: 18.5 - 24.9
- Overweight: 25.0 - 29.9
- Obese: 30.0 and above
High BMI is a major driving factor for metabolic syndrome, diabetes, and heart disease.

## Sleep Hygiene
Adults require 7-9 hours of sleep per night. 
Chronic sleep deprivation (under 6 hours) increases cortisol (stress hormone), which can elevate blood pressure and fasting glucose levels.

## Biological Age
Biological age is a concept used to describe how old your cells and tissues act, rather than your chronological age in years.
Improving cardiovascular health, reducing BMI, and quitting smoking can significantly reduce your biological age.
