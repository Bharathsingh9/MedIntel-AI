# Diabetes Management Guidelines

## Understanding HbA1c
HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin) measures your average blood sugar levels over the past 3 months.
- Normal: Below 5.7%
- Prediabetes: 5.7% to 6.4%
- Diabetes: 6.5% or higher
High HbA1c significantly increases the risk of cardiovascular complications and neuropathy.

## Fasting Glucose
Fasting blood glucose measures sugar in your blood after an overnight fast.
- Normal: 99 mg/dL or lower
- Prediabetes: 100 to 125 mg/dL
- Diabetes: 126 mg/dL or higher

## Lifestyle Interventions
To reduce diabetes risk:
1. Weight Management: Losing 5-7% of body weight reduces risk by up to 58%.
2. Physical Activity: Aim for 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise per week.
3. Nutrition: Focus on whole grains, lean proteins, and fiber. Reduce simple carbohydrates and sugary drinks.
